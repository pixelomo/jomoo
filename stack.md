Stack

Next.js: framework.
Clerk: User authentication and account management (sign up, sign in, sign out).
Supabase: Database for registrations, warranty records, and user info.
Sanity: CMS for products and page content, easy to use wysiwyg editor.
Cloudinary: Image storage for uploads such as receipts and serial numbers.
Resend: Service for sending transactional emails.
next-intl: Internationalization for Chinese and English language support.
Tailwind CSS: CSS framework.
Vercel: Hosting and deployment.
